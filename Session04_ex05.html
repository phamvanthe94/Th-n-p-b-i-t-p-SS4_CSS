What is CSS?

Cascading Style Sheets(CSS) is used to fomat the layout of a webpage.

With CSS, you can control the color,font,the size of text, the spacing bettween elements, how elements are positioned and laid out, what background images or background colors are to be used, different displays for different devices and screen sizes, and much more!

Tip: The word cascading means that a style applied to a parent element will also apply to all children elements whithin the body will also get the same color (unless you specify something else)!
